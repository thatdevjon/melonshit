How to become an affiliate

If you're looking to make some extra money, we recommend you join our affiliate program.

When signed up, you will get your unique affiliate link. You can share and distribute your link, and every time someone purchases something within 90 days after visiting our website through your unique link you will be paid 20% of what they paid us (This is a one-time commission)!

There is no limit to how many customers or orders you can refer to us. 
 
To become an affiliate:
1: Log into the client panel by clicking here.
2: On the top menu, click on 'Affiliates' then click on 'Activate Affiliate Account.'
 
That's it!
