Free Transfers

MelonCube provides free transfers for Minecraft servers hosted with other hosts. All of your files including worlds, plugins and config files are moved so that your server is exactly the same as with your old host.

Please submit a support ticket with your old FTP details (Host, Username, Password, Port) and ask us to transfer the old files to your new server with us.
