How to add a sub-account

If you want someone else to have (limited) access to your account and functions without giving them your details, you can add them as a joint user to your main account.

1. Log into the client area.
2. Then go to: Welcome, Name > Edit Account Details > Contacts / Sub-Accounts
3. On this page you will be able to create or remove contacts on your account.
4. To mange what permissions the sub-account will have tick the Activate Sub-account check box.

Please be careful with the permissions you give to someone else. We are not responsible for what your contacts do with your account. Make sure you trust the person you give access to.
