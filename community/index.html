Our community first approach

Many hosting companies tend to focus on themselves, whilst having high profit margins. At MelonCube, we balance our profit with giving back by sponsoring the Minecraft community. We provide monetary compensation alongside free or discounted Minecraft hosting to bring players closer to YouTubers and Twitchers.

  YouTube

Minecraft-related YouTube applicants must meet or exceed the following requirements:

  • 5,000 subscribers
  • 100,000 total views
  • regular uploads in the past 3 months

By being sponsored, your task will be to effectively promote our services in your video and description.

As compensation, we offer a negotiable base rate, in addition to performance-based commission for every sale.

  Twitch

Minecraft-related Twitch applicants must meet or exceed the following requirements:

  • 250 watching
  • 1,000 followers
  • regular streaming schedule

By being sponsored, your task will be to effectively promote our services via your stream and description.

As compensation, we offer a negotiable base rate, in addition to performance-based commission for every sale.

  Websites

Minecraft-related website owners must meet or exceed the following requirements:

  • 15,000 unique visitors per month
  • 75,000 page views per month
  • Minecraft related site or forum

MelonCube helps fund websites by providing free virtual or dedicated hosting for large websites, or monetary payment for placing banners on the website.

Our community team will require Google Analytics data to verify requirements.
